Gathering Data

I primarily collected my data from Zelda Dungeon, a fan made wiki page that has pages dedicated to cataloging the different items from almost every game in the Zelda series. There were a few instances where Zelda Dungeon did not have an items page for a game, so I used various other sources that listed the items found in the game, which are listed on this page.

Note On Data Collection

As I was gathering my data, I had to make some decisions about what items I would or would not include, and so not every single item in the Zelda series is included in this data. For example, I opted to exclude most items that were a part of trade sequences (like flowers in Wind Waker) or items whose' sole purpose was to be collected then traded in for rewards (like the gold skulltulas in Ocarina of Time). There were also instances where I chose to exclude upgrades for certain items, while including other items’ upgraded counterparts. Furthermore, some items across different games save the same purpose while having slightly different names (such as the hookshot being called the clawshot in Twilight Princess and Skyward Sword). I opted to group these items under one name, unless their function and look varied enough that I decided it should be considered its own unique item (the deku leaf, sailcloth, and paraglider all function similarly, but are all distinct enough to deserve their own categories). In other instances, I grouped several items into a singular type of item (rather than listing every single mask you can obtain in Majora’s Mask, I grouped all of them together under “Masks”). All of this should be taken into consideration when viewing the data.
